How to Get Your Website on Google

Getting your website on Google is not as hard as you think, it's actually simple.

Head Over To Google Search Console

In order to get your website online youre going to want to head over to Google Search Console

Their you can follow the process which will initially ask you to type in the domain of your website.

Google Will Ask You To Verify Your Domain

From their Google will check it's availibailty. If is currently not in use in their system they'll provide you with a .TXT file that you'll need to place in the domain name settings of your website. This file has a unique code that Google will be able to detect and veryfiy that the domain you submitted does infact belong to you.

Verify Your Domain With Google Search Console

Their are several ways to verify your domain, the easiest for me has been the way google initally suggests.

Expect To Wait About A Day

This process can take about a day to complete so be a little bit patient. Once done you can expect to start seeing analytics about how Google is using your website.
